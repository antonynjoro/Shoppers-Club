The Process Is Simple

Delivering your Shopping from the USA to Kenya is easy when you shop with the Shoppers Club. The process is hassle free and works with your convenience in mind.

Download the Shoppers Club Extension to your Firefox or Chrome Browser

You can download and install the shoppers Club Shopping Extensions on your browser on a computer. This will allow you to shop on your favorite online shops and add your products to the shoppers club Shopping cart.

Order Your Product on any of your favorite online shops. 

Order your product with any of the online shops: Amazon, Macy's, Victoria's Secret, Toys R Us, by adding the products to the Online shop's cart.  Once you are done, using the bar at the top of your shopping window, Click CheckOut with Shoppers's Club. Clicking this will redirect you to this website.

Pay for your products in Kenya Shillings

Once you arrive on this website, the products you ordered will be listed on the website with the prices in Kenyan Shilings. You will also see the total shipping fees and the excise duty that we will pay to KRA for the products.  Confirm the order by clicking the payment button and make your payment using MPesa or PayPal.  You will then be prompted to confirm your delivery location in Kenya and receive an estimated time of arrival.

Receive Your Products in Kenya

It usually takes us about 2 weeks to deliver the product you have ordered. We will call you once your product is ready to be delivered. We will deliver your package to any place within Nairobi and Thika at no extra charge. However, if you live further away, we will get the product to you at a minimal fee.
